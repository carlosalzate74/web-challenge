Since Latitud is the angular distance from the Equator, from the Latitude vs. Temperature chart and based on the sample on over 500 cities, we can say the lower the Latitude, the higher the Temperature. In human words, cities closer to the Equator tend to be hotter.

There are variations, though, as other factors such as elevation, ocean currents, and precipitation affect climate patterns.
